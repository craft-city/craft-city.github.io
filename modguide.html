Craft-City Moderator Guide

Written by Train | Last Revised 6/22/16 11:45 PM

Rules

Make sure to review the rules here.

Clarifications:

  • Greifing is allowed if it is outside of a sponge protection. Players are responsible for the own builds. If you recieve a greifing complaint and it is not inside a protected area, politely explain to them that they must claim their area for it to be protected.
  • The word "hell" does not fall under the profanity ban unless it is abused (i.e. "go to hell")

Offenses

Profanity: 15 minute mute for minor offense, 30 for major.
Exploits/Glitches: DO NOT BAN, notify me, and I will look into the situation. It is hard to prove these kinds of claims.
Advertising: If it is a major advertisement, (i.e. logging on and spamming the IP of another server), ban the player immediately. If it is simply a regular player putting the IP of another server in the chat, politely ask them to stop.
Impersonation: Use /realname [nick] to determine the real username of the player. If they are impersonating somebody else, mute them for 5 minutes, and politely ask them to change back their nickname.
Spamming: If the player is using caps lock too much, ask them to stop. If it continues, mute them for 15 minutes. If they are advertising, refer to the advertisement line above.
Mods: If you feel that a person is using a mod that gives them advantage over other players, notify me. I will sort out the issue.
Religion/Politics: If a player is discussing religion/politics, politely ask them to stop.
Racism/Sexism: If a minor offense, mute for 30 minutes. If major, ban immediately.
PvP Logging: Jail for 15 minutes.

Commands

Mute: /mute [player] [time]m, then message the player with the reason (/m [player] [reason]). Ex: /mute TrainGaming 5m (DON'T FORGET THE m, as it specifies minutes)
Jail: /jail [player] main [time]. Ex: /jail TrainGaming main 5m (DON'T FORGET THE m, as it specifies minutes)
Ban: /ban [player] [reason]
Don't forget to include a time, and always provide a reason.
