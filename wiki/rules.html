Minecraft & TeamSpeak Server Rules

By logging onto Craft-City, you agree to abide by the rules listed on this page.

  • Global Rules (These apply everywhere)
    • Respect all players and staff members.
    • Do not use profanity in the chat.
    • Do not use exploits/glitches to damage any part of the server.
    • Advertising is not allowed in any form.
    • Do not impersonate (i.e. putting someones username in your nickname)
    • Do not spam the chat (random letters, excessive caps, repeat messages)
    • Do not use mods that improve your capabilities above other players. Aesthetic/Graphics performance mods such as Optifine and Shaders are allowed.
    • Discussion of religion/politics is not allowed.
    • Homophobia/Racism/Sexism/any other "ism" that could offend someone is strictly prohibited.
    • Using alternate accounts to circumvent bans or to make more money is strictly prohibited. In other words, one account per person.
  • PvP Arena Rules
    • Do not logout while in the active PvP area. (Note: The server logs report whether logouts are network errors or actual logouts, so don't try to claim a PvP log was a network error when it really wasn't)
    • Please remember to be respectful.

Staff members can and will take action against players who do not abide by these rules. Rules are subject to change at any time.
