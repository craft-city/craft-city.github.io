Survival: City Plots

Within the city, there are a number of small plots that run on a rent-based system. Each has a sign that specifies the name of the plot, the price, and the rental length. To rent a lot, simply type /buyregion (don't worry, it still constitutes as rental mode) and then right click the sign.


To add somebody to your plot, type /region addmember [plotname] [username]


To automatically renew your rental, type /buyregion autorenew true
