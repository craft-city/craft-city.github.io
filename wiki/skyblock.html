Skyblock

Skyblock is a very enjoyable (and addictive) classic minigame. Whether you want to start an island on your own or play with friends, you will have a good time.


/is create - Create an island

/is delete - Removes your island forever

/is info - Information about your island

/is home - Teleport to your island

/is expell - Removes unwanted players from island

/is visit [player] - Sends request to visit another player's island

/is accept - Accepts visit request

/is rank - Shows your island rank

/challenges list - Lists skyblock challenges

/challenges complete [id] - Completes a skyblock challenge on the list


/is add [player] - Adds a player to your island

/is remove [player] - Removes player from island

/is leave - Leaves another player's island


/is biomelist - Lists available biomes

/is biomeset [biome] - Sets island biome

/is biomeshow - Shows current island biome
